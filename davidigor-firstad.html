David Igor Rumyantsev

Bio:

David Igor Rumyantsev, 24, born and raised in Lviv, Ukraine.
“I’ve always been fascinated by beauty,” David says.

“Cinema taught me that beauty can be captured - and that only film can truly reveal what beauty means.” Deeply inspired by the bold spirit and innovation of the French New Wave, David sees filmmaking as both an art and a way of understanding the world.
