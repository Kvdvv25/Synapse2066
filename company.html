About Us

Who We Are

SYNAPSE Corporation (commonly stylised as SYNAPSE) is a multinational neurotechnology and biotechnology conglomerate founded in 2041. The company is best known for developing the Neuro-Link series of neural implants and for its extensive influence in cognitive computing, data integration, and neural-assistive medical systems throughout the mid-21st century. SYNAPSE is headquartered in Megazone, with operations in over 80 countries as of 2066. It is considered a “Chaebol” a family controlled conglomerate.

History

2041 - 2050:

Origins and Early Growth

SYNAPSE began as a medical research startup focused on software designed to assist patients with degenerative neurological disorders. Early prototypes of what would later become the Neuro-Link Interface were initially created as external devices. By 2047, SYNAPSE released the Neuro-Link V1, the first commercially available implanted neural interface for cognitive enhancement and pain regulation.

2050 - 2066:


Global Expansion

Through a series of mergers and acquisitions, SYNAPSE expanded rapidly into military contracting, healthcare infrastructure, and data-storage services. The company’s Neuro-Link V3 and V4 models became widely adopted for both medical and civilian use. During this period, several activist groups and independent researchers raised concerns regarding data privacy, user tracking, and the corporation’s growing political influence.

2060 - 2066:

Controversies & Allegations

In 2063, leaked files allegedly showing unauthorized human trials related to “neural synchronization experiments” appeared online. SYNAPSE denied the authenticity of the materials, stating they were fabricated by former employees. In 2064, two SYNAPSE research contractors, Leo and Anna reportedly attempted to whistleblow internal research data. Both individuals disappeared shortly after the incident. While SYNAPSE never publicly acknowledged involvement, several independent sources allege that the corporation conducted a covert retrieval operation targeting the pair. Anna was believed to have been abducted following audio logs recovered by underground networks. Some speculate she is being held inside a SYNAPSE-controlled neural simulation environment, though this has never been confirmed. Leo remains unaccounted for and is the subject of an ongoing international warrant for unauthorized breaches into SYNAPSE server infrastructure. Multiple intrusion attempts traced to his identifier have been recorded since 2065.

Technology

Neurolink Interface

The Neuro-Link line is SYNAPSE’s flagship neural implant technology. It provides:

  • sensory regulation
  • pain suppression
  • cognitive support
  • neural-to-network communication

Later generations included experimental features related to cross-neural data transmission, though SYNAPSE denies pursuing mind-to-mind synchronization research.

Neural Containment Simulation

(Unverified)

Some whistleblower accounts reference a closed neural ecosystem allegedly used to preserve or stabilize compromised Neuro-Link users. SYNAPSE maintains that no such platform exists.

Neuro-Spectra scan visualizing real-time neural pathways through Neuro-Link.

Public Reception

SYNAPSE holds a dominant market position and maintains high approval ratings in medical and commercial sectors.
However, the corporation is a frequent subject of criticism regarding:

  • alleged user surveillance
  • aggressive corporate security tactics
  • potential human rights violations
  • lack of transparency in neural-research programs

In Popular Culture

SYNAPSE is often depicted in media as a symbol of technological overreach and government- corporate entanglement. The unexplained disappearance of Leo and Anna has become a recurring subject of documentaries, conspiracy forums, and investigative journalism.
